About Me

I'm a data analyst with a Business Administration background from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria. My work sits at the intersection of data and business — I care about asking the right questions, not just building the fanciest charts.

My experience spans data annotation at Outlier, freelance data analysis, virtual assistance, and customer-facing roles — giving me a practical, business-first perspective on data problems. I've worked with real datasets in Excel and Power BI, and I'm currently expanding into SQL and Tableau.
